# Debrae's Banana Bread

Taken from
Debrae

---
## Specifications
- Total time: 70 mins
- Prep time: 10 mins
- Bake time: 60 mins
- Yield: 1 loaf bread
- Oven temp: 350 F

---
## Ingredients

- 1 1/3 cup flour
- 2 1/2 tsp baking powder
- 1/2 tsp salt
- 1/3 cup oil or butter
- 2/3 cup sugar
- 3/4 tbsp lemon rind
- 2 eggs
- 1 1/4 cup mashed banana

---
## Directions

- Mix together flour, baking powder, and salt. 
- Add in other ingredients
- Mix
- Bake on a greased loaf pan.
