# New York Style Cheesecake

Taken from
Merritt's Cookbook

---
## Specifications
- Total time: 6-7 hours
- Prep time: 30 mins
- Bake time: 50-60 mins
- Yield: 1 cheesecake
- Oven temp: 350 F

---
## Ingredients

Crust
- 1 cup Graham crackers
- 1/2 cup (1/2 stick) butter, melted
- 1/4 cup white sugar

Filling
- 4 packages (8 oz) cream cheese, softened
- 1 cup white sugar
- 3 tbsp flour
- 4 large eggs
- 1 cup sour cream
- 2 tsp vanilla

Fresh fruit to decorate if desired.

---
## Directions

- Butter a 9 in spring form pan. Mix crumbs, butter, and sugar in a bowl. Press into bottom and sides of pan. Bake for 8-10 minutes.Cool completely.

Filling
- Beat cream cheese, sugar, and flour into a bowl until creamy.
- Add in eggs, one at a time. Mix well.
- Add in sour cream and vanilla. Spoon filling into crust.
- Bake for 50-60 minutes and refrigerate for 6 hours.
